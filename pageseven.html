Redesign: champion.gg

UI/UX, App

A redesign of a website that I used when playing League of Legends. The mobile version of champion.gg was not created for a mobile layout so I decided to create a mock design of what it potentially could of have. I focused on the important stats/information that a player would need to have and skipped out on the extra information that the desktop site had. Created in Adobe Xd and Photoshop.
